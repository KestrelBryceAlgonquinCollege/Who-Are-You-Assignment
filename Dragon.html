Nonbinary Dragon



Medium:

Traditional Art – Pen & Paper

Method:

Hand Drawn

Motivation:

I wanted to design a powerful, nonbinary character.

Challenges:

I feel that I’ve been spoiled by digital art and its individual layers. When sketching the final image of the dragon, I put the flag in the background, the right wing in front of it, and the left wing in front of both. While this depth looks good in the final product, drawing all three of these layers on the same “layer” of paper was extremely frustrating.

Decisions:

Over three iterations, I determined that having the dragon holding the flag with their tail would show possessiveness, and their body posture should be fiercely protecting it. I also decided a landscape version would fit this portfolio better.
