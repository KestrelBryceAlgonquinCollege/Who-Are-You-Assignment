Medium:

Digital Art

Method:

Hand Drawn

Motivation:

I wanted to design an RPG with nonbinary protagonists.

Challenges:

The Bow’s animal association was difficult. I wanted it to be a bird as arrows have feathers, but there’s not a lot of physical space to allow characterization while still maintaining the form of a bow.

Decisions:

I chose to use enchanted items for members of the adventuring party as they inherently lack gender as well as selecting mostly colours that are gender neutral.
